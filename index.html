About me

Hi, my name is Coy Sanders and I am a Cloud Engineer currently working for Hewlett Packard Enterprise. I have over six years of experience working in various enterprise-scale cloud environments where I perform support from the Application Level (UI, upgrades) down to the Hardware/OS Level (Linux, RAID, Xen/KVM Virtualization).

In 2008 I earned a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo which encompasses both Electrical Engineering and Computer Science disciplines together. Since then I have been learning through work experience and self-teaching myself skills on various layers of the stack on the side. I obtained the Linux+/LPIC-1 Certifications from CompTIA in 2015 and currently I am rekindling my passion to get back into programming using FreeCodeCamp's web-development tracks.

In July 2016 I earned the Front-End Certification from FreeCodeCamp which is one of three certifications required to participate in nonprofit projects to work on. The goal is to obtain a Full Stack Development Certification which is earned after the three certifications in Front-End, Back-End, and Data Visualization are obtained and several projects for non-profits have been completed. I am also interested in continuing to persue other certifications on both CompTIA's track (A+, Network+, Security+) as well as others such as RedHat certifications. The ultimate goal is becoming a true Full-Stack Engineer who is capable of building and supporting components on all layers.
